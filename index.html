About Thanima

“Festivals promote diversity, they bring neighbours into dialogue, they increase creativity, they offer opportunities for civic pride, they improve our general psychological well-being. In short, they make cities better places to live.” – David Binder

Essentially, the harvest festival of Kerala brings about a general sense of unity among all the Malayalis, overcoming barriers, obstacles and boundaries, all of us come together as one to celebrate this joyous occasion. This harvesting festival represents the home-coming of the mythical demon-king Maveli, celebrated on the first day of the Chingam month, which is the first month in the Malayalam calendar, Kollavarsham. The Onam celebrations, here at VIT, is celebrated through ‘Thanima’. Thanima was first conducted in the year 2002 and thus was set into motion, the legacy of this wonderful and auspicious celebration. Each year our celebrations are aggrandized by the presence of dignified personalities such as Vineeth Sreenivasan, Swetha Menon, Shri. K. B. Ganesh Kumar, Deepak Dev, Shri. G Karthikeyan, Siddhi Mahajankatti, Vayalar Ravi (MP), Marina Michael Kurisingal etc. The Thanima festivities are also accompanied by various Sporting and Cultural events that boost up morale, companionship, sportsmanship, creativity and togetherness of the entire community. It is a visual treat to witness all these art forms and tradition being exhibited in all its vigour in such a grand stage. This visual spectacle is also a ground for the most renowned Onasadhya which is the grand feast, quite like no other. The multitude of flavours that strike our taste buds and sends it into a nostalgic frenzy is one of the most delightful parts of the Thanima celebration. Thanima brings together this entire community and gives us the opportunity to explore how as malayalis we should be proud about our culture. Besides being a celebration, Thanima has found its way into becoming a pillar of support for those who are in need which includes medical aids during floods, activities such as tree planting and much more.

Thanima 2020

As the tale goes, Maveli was granted a single wish from Vishnu – To come forth to his kingdom and see his people. The people of the Kerala.

Thus follows the legend of Onam – The Apex of Festivities in our God’s Own Country, where people come together as one, united by the spirit of Onam.

Inculcating the spirit of this legend, we, the students of VIT have organised Onam celebrations almost every year. This helps in bringing together the entire Malayali community under one roof. This year has been nothing but a bag of disheartening surprises and as the curtains drop on all the traditional festivities and people become glued to the incessant inevitability of dwindling hope and the ever so escalating air of eerie despair, we have charted out the plan to revive the spirits and to break this chain of inactivity.

Thanima 2k20 Live embodies the spirit of previous Thanima yet puts forth the entirely avant-garde concept of the Event being conducted online. It shall completely shatter the preconceived notions of online events and will set a new standard for such events in the future. All the Events and competitions shall be completely online and will be conducted in a pattern wherein people don’t feel threatened about their safety. All Thanima 2k20 events and competitions shall have rewards and manifest the feeling of recouping ethos of what Onam means to each one of us. Although we are separated, locked away in different corners, all of us shall be brought together on this festive occasion to enlighten our spirits through the staggering exhibition of Dance, Music, Art and culture.

Let this festive season of colours, lights, happiness and oneness be scintillating once again and let it not be ‘masked’ behind this menacing visitor. The radiance of Thanima 2020 shall be the beacon of hope for all VIT students in these testing times.

Contests

Here is a brief summary of our Contests. Detailed description will be announced soon.

  • Ponnonam Poovonam - പൊന്നോണം പൂവോണം

    Pookalam or the flower carpet has always been an integral part of the Onam tradition. Capture the very essence of this colorful ensemble of flowers in a photograph and win exciting prizes.

  • Onachamayam - ഓണച്ചമയം

    Being the best dressed is a compliment we all love to hear. So this Thanima , get your settu sarees, mundu and other ethnic wears, and enroll in this competition. Send your picture and join in the run to become the best traditionally dressed boy and girl.

  • Ila Ready - ഇല റെഡി

    Way to a person’s heart is through their stomach and for us Malayalees, traditional SADHYA is definitely the way. This Onam, record a video presenting the mouth-watering sadhya and get yourself a chance to win an amazing prize.

  • Mr & Ms Thanima 2020

    Comprising of a series of rounds, Mr and Ms Thanima aims at finding individuals who have the right combination of beauty, skills and personality.

  • Treasure Hunt

    Here as the classic treasure hunt game takes up an online platform, the group of contestants will be given a set of clues leading to the treasure. Gear up to solve puzzles, riddles and secret codes to find your treasure.

  • Recreation of Movie Scenes

    The Mollywood industry has been able to deliver a wide variety of movies. For this competition, the participants can recreate any movie scene of their choice. The one with the best resemblance wins. All movie buffs and actors out there, go for it.

  • Poster Making

    Posters are the depiction of one's idea in the most minimal, efficient, and attractive manner. Here's an opportunity for all of you to portray the very essence of ONAM either in a handmade or digital poster and get rewarded for your talent.

  • Caption Hunt

    Captions are what make or breaks a post. Put your sense of humor, wit, sarcasm, and creativity and come up with the most exciting caption for our post.

  • LUDO & PUBG

    This quarantine lockdown has improved all our gaming skills, isn’t it? Call up your gang, play your favorite game online( LUDO or PUBG), and win some great prizes( maybe even a chicken dinner!).

  • Lucky Draw

    Register for Thanima and get a chance to be one of the lucky winners.

  • Naadan Pattu

    Brief description of the game in around 100 words

  • Dance

    Dance is said to be the hidden language of soul .This thanima, groove to the tunes of some of the classic Malayalam songs in a style of your choice. Get ready to win by doing what you love.
