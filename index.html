About Thanima

“ It is said that Maveli was granted one wish by Lord Vishnu and that was to see his people once every year ". We the students of VIT made sure that the sight that he saw was grand every time he visited.

Just as sweetness cannot be separated from honey, Onam cannot be separated from a malayalis heart. This harvest festival of Kerala brings divine message of unity in diversity. Onam is celebrated with all its vigor in VIT campus through “Thanima”. Thanima was first conducted in VIT in the year 2002. Each year the function is graced by the presence of dignified personality including Vineeth Sreenivasan, Swetha Menon, Shri.K.B. Ganesh Kumar, Deepak Dev, Shri G Karthikeyan. Sports and the Pookalam competition are the pre events of Thanima. These events bolsters the sportsmanship, team spirit and creativity of the peers. The cultural events provides an opportunity to showcase the multifaceted talents of the students. Finally, the sumptuous Onasadhya evokes the nostalgia for the feast that relished out taste buds at home. Thanima provides a platform for all malayalees to come together and share a common heritage and pride: the pride of being a Malayali, a festival that unites all the people regardless of caste and religion. What started out as an aspiration to celebrate oneness, has now grown into united efforts to reach out to those in need. Thanima is not just a celebration, over the years Thanima has been able to bring about positive changes in the society through tree plantations, medical aids and much more.

Ms.Deepa Tom

Ex-Faculty & Counsellor

Thanima is a recreation of Onam celebration in VIT, which focuses on joy, happiness and togetherness among students from all faith and caste. I still remember my first thanima which was in 2016, the goosebumps when I heard Malayalam hit songs in a different state after a long time elicited the malayali in me. It's an incarnation of Kerala with Pookkalam, tug of war, Thiruvathira, other cultural events and sadhya which is my favorite part... in all it's a festive day. The vibe and bond that I got from thanima is still my strength even after leaving VIT.
